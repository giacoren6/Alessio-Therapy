Everything you need to know before booking

What is Hybrid Therapy?

Hybrid Therapy is a unique blend of techniques that combinesdeep tissue massage, sports massage, assisted stretching, andmobility-focused bodywork.Not just about relieving pain it's about helping your body move better, feel lighter, and reconnect emotionally and physically.

Do I need to be flexible or athletic to book a session?

Not at all! BeKindBloom is forevery body,whether you’re an athlete, a creative, or someone who sits at a desk all day. Each session is tailored to your level of movement, comfort, and goals.

What should I wear?

Wear something comfortable you can move in, like gym or yoga clothes. For some treatments (like massage), you'll be covered with a towel or blanket. For mobility or stretch-based sessions, you’ll stay dressed and move gently throughout.

Can I combine massage and stretch therapy in one session?

Yes, that’s exactly what Hybrid Therapy is for. I’ll intuitively combine methods based on how your body feels on the day. You don’t need to choose in advance unless you have a preference.

I’ve never had a treatment before — is it painful?

No. My work isdeep, but never forceful.You’re always in control, and we work together to find the right balance between effective release and gentle care.

Do you work with injuries or medical conditions?

If you’re recovering from an injury, please let me know before booking. I’m currently studying physiotherapy and use safe, informed techniques, but I may refer you to a specialist if needed.

How do I book a session?

You can book directly through the Contact and Booking section page, or feel free to DM me on Instagram @BeKindBloom for last-minute availability or questions.

Where are you based?

BeKindBloom collaborates with a range ofstudio spaces across London and the UK,offering treatments in carefully selected wellness locations that align with our values of softness, movement, and emotional care.

Follow @BeKindBloom or check the booking page to see where I’m currently working from.

Do you offer mobile treatments or corporate bookings?

Yes! I offer on-site treatments, workplace wellness sessions, and event services. Reach out via the contact form or email to create a custom package.
