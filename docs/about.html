BeKindBloom

I’m Alessio — a hybrid therapist and founder of BeKindBloom, blending sports massage, stretch therapy, mobility coaching, and emotional wellness into one fluid, body-led experience. My journey started in hospitality, where I learned the art of human connection. I loved creating meaningful experiences, but something in me craved deeper healing — a way to care for people not just with service, but with presence, touch, and movement. That inner pull led me to retrain in massage therapy, dive into mobility and stretch therapy, and begin my path in Physiotherapy. Over time, I stepped into bodywork and became what I now call a hybrid therapist — someone who brings together manual therapy, movement, softness, and emotional presence to support people in feeling better, freer, and more connected to themselves. At BeKindBloom, I bring together everything I’ve learned — from deep tissue to assisted stretching, emotional holding to scientific rehab — to offer a space where healing feels personal, grounded, and real.

My approach is rooted in:

  • Mobility and flexibility trainingto help you move freely and prevent injury.
  • Sports massage therapyto release muscular tension and aid recovery.
  • Stretch therapyto open up space in the body and calm the nervous system.
  • A growing foundation in physiotherapy, which guides my understanding of rehab and body mechanics
  • And above all, a deep belief inemotional wellness through body awareness and gentle care

I work with creatives, athletes, new mothers, busy professionals, and anyone who feels disconnected from their body and wants to come home to it again. Whether you’re here for recovery, relaxation, or reconnection — welcome.This is a space to soften, stretch, and bloom.
